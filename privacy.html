Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to Sri Mahalingeshwara Temple, Padubelman

At Sri Mahalingeshwara Temple, Padubelman, we deeply respect the trust you place in us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website, seva bookings, and WhatsApp communication.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, and seva or donation details when you voluntarily share them with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Send temple updates, festival notifications, and seva schedules
  • Provide donation receipts or acknowledgments
  • Respond to queries or requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your details. Only authorized temple trust members may access your data for religious and administrative purposes.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage devotees to share details responsibly.

5. Opt-Out

You may opt out of updates anytime by contacting us at padubelmantemple@gmail.com.
